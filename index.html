CANADIAN CONQUEST

About the Game:

Welcome to the Canadian Conquest!
Canada is a land of diversity, inclusion, and polite citizens.
It is a country that has it all - from mountains to valleys, from lakes to islands, from moose to Canadian geese.
Canada has been home to many iconic individuals such as Terry Fox, Alexander Graham Bell, Aubrey Drake Graham, and Sandra Oh.
From coast to coast, Canada is united through equality, safety and peacefulness, and a strong foundational democracy, all of which are reflected in the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms.
This country is also well known for its Northern Lights, maple syrup, and Tim Hortons.
This game aims to provide a snapshot of Canada in a fun way!

How to Play:

  • On the next screen, you will get to pick your avatar from a list of notable Canadian symbols. Once an avatar is selected, you can no longer change it after the game begins.
  • In order to move your character, you must click the "roll die" button located under the board.
  • Once you roll the die, your character will move a certain number of squares as specified on the die.
  • If you feel the need to restart during any point in the game, click on the Title to relaunch the game.
  • Your objective is to get to the last square on the course.

To begin the game click "LET'S SELECT AN AVATAR!" button below to proceed and select your character.
